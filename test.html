About Me

I am an experienced manager, life coach, public speaker, and full-stack developer. I spent 7 years on a team growing martial arts school in Massachusetts. I was in charge of student acquisition, retention, sales, and quality assurance in the classroom.

One thing that always frustrated me was working with apps that couldn't keep up with the fast pace I needed. I spoke with other managers and business owners, and realized how much room there is for improvement in application experiance and speed. Between my computer engineering knowledge and my Bachelor's degree in Communications, I hope to be the missing link between customer and developer.

A fun fact about me is my can-do attitude sometimes gets the better of me. I once offered to play a bride down the isle because her pianist quit and the wedding was in two weeks. The problem is I never played piano before. I called the church pianist who was horrified. We practiced every day for two weeks. Luckily, I pulled it off and learned never to to say yes in that particular dilemna again.
