About Me

Ethan Wager is an experienced manager, life coach, public speaker, and more recently, full-stack developer. He spent 7 years on a team growing a set of martial arts school in Massachusetts. He was in charge of student acquisition, retention, sales, and quality assurance in the classroom.

One thing that always frustrated Ethan was working with apps that couldn't keep up. He spoke with other business owners and found how common it was to have apps that were quirky, unwieldy, and slow. Excited to help innovate and fix the great divide between business owners and their apps, he went back to school to become a full-stack developer. As a developer with an bacholers in Communications, he hopes to be the missing link on a app development team.

A fun fact about Ethan is his can-do attitude sometimes gets the best of him. He once offered to play a bride down the isle because her pianist quit and the wedding was in two weeks. The problem is he never played piano before. He called the church pianist who was horrified and they practiced every day for two weeks. Luckily, he pulled it off and learned never to to say yes in that particular dilemna again.
