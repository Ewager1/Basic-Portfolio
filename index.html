About Me

Nice to meet you! I am a passionate full stack developer interested in modernizing business applications. Like many others, I was often frustrated with apps that were slow and unintuitive. As a fitness manager, I began studying fitness systems such as Perfect-mind, mind-body, punch-pass, clear, and club-ready to grasp what works and what doesn’t from a user perspective. I went back to school to learn full stack developing to help bridge the gap and pursue other programming interests.

My non-programming background includes Bachelors in Corporate Communications from Bridgewater University. I focused mostly on leadership communications. I’m very interested about the gap between what people mean to say and what people actually hear. I also managed several schools for Elite Freestyle Karate over 7 years. When I left, I had over 250 clients and was in charge of a small and effective team. I believe my team management experience will be one of my strongest assets with any company I team up with.

Fun Story: My positive attitude sometimes gets the better of me. For example, I once offered to play a bride down the aisle because her pianist quit two weeks before the wedding. I just felt bad and really wanted to help. The problem is, I never played piano before. I called the church pianist, borrowed some equipment from my friends, and practiced every day for two weeks. Luckily, the wedding went off perfectly. I told the bride a few weeks later and we had a good laugh.
